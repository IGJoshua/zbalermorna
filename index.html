Zbalermorna

Zbalermorna is a diacritic orthography for Lojban, created by la kmir. A couple diferent versions of the write-ups exist in the wild, and this page is an attempt to consolodate all of the information on Zbalermorna, and provide resources for fonts, IMEs, and other software.

The page itself is hosted on Github pages through Jack's fork of la kmir's official repo - most of the content here should be considered a unofficial, and while some feedback on the ideas proposed has been collected from la kmir, a lot of it is still actively being worked on.
