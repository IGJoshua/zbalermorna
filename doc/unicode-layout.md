### Punctuation Key

- `|`   - {slakabu} syllabalic
- `/`   - tone mark: up
- `\`   - tone mark: down
- `/\`  - tone mark: up-down
- `\/`  - tone mark: down-up
- `!`   - {bahebu} emphasis mark
- `!!`  - {bahebu} double emphasis mark
- `!!!` - {bahebu} triple emphasis mark
- `___` - stretch
- `|--` - {smajibu} silence dash [initial]
- `---` - {smajibu} silence dash [medial]
- `--|` - {smajibu} silence dash [final]
- `|-|` - {smajibu} silence dash [isolated]
